You're limited to waiting for creators to find you, and when they do, you have no data to know if they actually drive sales. AfterShip gives you the search, the outreach, and the attribution so every decision is backed by performance data, not gut feel. "We're already getting creator applications through the Affiliate Center." +
Inbound applications are a good sign — it means there's demand. But inbound-only means you only see creators who already know you. AfterShip lets you find the creators your competitors haven't reached yet, filter by verified GMV, and reach them at scale. Most brands that grow to 200+ active affiliates do it through proactive outreach, not waiting.
"We don't have budget for another platform right now." +
Totally fair. How many hours a week is your team spending on manual creator outreach and tracking right now? Most teams spend 10+ hours on tasks AfterShip automates completely. The question is whether the time cost and missed GMV from slower activation is more or less than the platform fee.
